# **App Name**: PortfolioPro

## Core Features:

- Work Showcase: Display your portfolio items (web development and social media content) with images, descriptions, and links to live projects.
- AI-Powered Portfolio Review Tool: An AI powered tool that reviews the work samples added by the user and provides suggestions for improving the quality of the portfolio (titles, tags, and descriptions), helping users improve SEO and general ranking.
- Service Overview: Describe your web development and social media content creation services, highlighting key benefits and unique selling points.
- Contact Form: Allow potential clients to send you messages directly through the portfolio site.
- Testimonials Section: Showcase positive feedback from previous clients to build trust and credibility.
- About Me: Provide information about your professional background, skills, and experience.
- Social Media Links: Integrate links to your professional social media profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Slate blue (#5B8DAD) to evoke professionalism and creativity.
- Background color: Off-white (#F2F4F7) to ensure readability and a clean aesthetic.
- Accent color: Teal (#2AB1BC) to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif for a clean and modern feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif, used in combination with Poppins, enhances readability while maintaining a contemporary look.
- Use minimalist, line-based icons to represent different skills, services, and contact options.
- Employ a grid-based layout with ample whitespace for a balanced and uncluttered presentation.
